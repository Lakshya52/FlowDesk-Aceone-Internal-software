# 👋 Welcome to FlowDesk! (User Guide)

FlowDesk is your new central hub for all project work at Aceone. This guide will help you understand what the platform is, how to get started, and how to use its most powerful features.

---

## 🧐 What is FlowDesk?
Think of FlowDesk as your "Digital Office." Instead of using dozens of spreadsheets, sticky notes, and separate chat apps, FlowDesk brings everything together. It helps us keep track of who is doing what, when things are due, and allows us to collaborate in real-time.

---

## 🏁 Getting Started
When you first log in, you'll see the **Dashboard**. This is your command center. It shows you:
- **Active Projects**: How many projects are currently running.
- **My Tasks**: A shortcut to the work assigned specifically to you.
- **Recent Activity**: What has changed in your projects since you last checked.

---

## 📂 Working with Projects
Projects are where all the big work happens. In the **Projects** page, you'll see three tabs:
1. **Ongoing**: All projects we are currently working on.
2. **Completed**: Finished projects that we keep for reference.
3. **Recurring Blueprints**: "Templates" for repetitive work (see below).

### How to Create a New Project:
1. Click the **"New Project"** button.
2. Enter the **Title** and select the **Client/Company**.
3. Choose the **Priority** (Low, Medium, High, or Urgent).
4. Set a **Start Date** and a **Due Date** (or check "No due date" if there is no hard deadline).
5. Add the **Team Members** who will work on it.
6. Click **"Create Project"**.

---

## 🔄 Recurring Projects (The "Blueprint" System)
Do you have a project that repeats every day, week, or month? Use a **Blueprint**!
- Go to the **Recurring Blueprints** tab.
- Create a project and set it to "Recurring."
- Choose how often it repeats (e.g., Monthly).
- **The Magic**: FlowDesk will automatically create a "Fresh" copy of this project for you on the scheduled date, including all of its tasks. You never have to manually create the same project twice!

---

## ✅ Handling Tasks
Inside every project, there are **Tasks**. 
- **Checklists**: Each task can have its own checklist.
- **Status Stages**: Move your tasks from "Todo" to "In Progress" or "Review" as you work.
- **Review**: Once you finish a task, your manager will see it in the "Review" stage before it is officially completed.

---

## 🤝 Collaboration Tools
- **Chat**: Every project has its own chat. Use it to share updates, ask questions, and share files.
- **Canvas**: Need to brainstorm? The **Canvas** is like a digital whiteboard. You can create notes, move them around, and collaborate with your teammates visually.
- **AI Buddy**: Need help writing a description or analyzing a project? Ask your **AI Buddy**—the assistant icon in the bottom corner.

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Production
- **No Due Date**: If a project doesn't have a specific deadline, use the "No due date" checkbox. This keeps our calendar clean and avoids messy "1970" date errors.
- **Blueprint Updates**: If you update the tasks in a "Blueprint," they will be automatically included in the *next* instance that spawns.
- **Status Updates**: Keep your task status current! This helps your manager see progress without having to ask for manual reports.
